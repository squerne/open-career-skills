# Story Bank

One STAR story per file, named `YYYY-MM-short-slug.md` (year-month of when the story happened, roughly). The `star-story-extractor` skill writes these for you from braindumps; you can also add them by hand in this format:

```markdown
# [Punchy title, 8 words max]

**Tags:** stakeholder management, migration, crisis resolution

## Situation
[Context, company, scope, stakes.]

## Task
[The goal or responsibility you personally owned.]

## Action
[What YOU did. "I" voice, concrete verbs, real numbers.]

## Result
[The outcome, quantified wherever possible.]

## Open questions
[Optional: what would make this story stronger, e.g. "find the exact % improvement".]
```

Why bother: you will forget 80% of these details within a year, and behavioral interviews are won on details. Capture stories while they're fresh; the CV optimizer, cover-letter writer, and mock interviewer all mine this folder for evidence.

`example-story.md` shows a completed one. Delete it once you have real stories.
